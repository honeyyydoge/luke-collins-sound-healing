About

The journey behind the sound

Welcome to a sacred space of sound and vibration.

I'm Luke Collins, a sound healing practitioner based in Sydney. Through the ancient resonance of Himalayan gongs and singing bowls, I guide participants on transformative journeys of deep relaxation and inner harmony.

Each session is a unique experience, weaving together the powerful vibrations of bronze gongs and hand-hammered bowls to create a soundscape that washes away tension and invites profound stillness.

My instruments are sourced directly from the Himalayas, each carrying centuries of tradition and craftsmanship. The vibrations they produce work on a cellular level, helping to release blocked energy and restore balance to body, mind, and spirit.

The Instruments

Himalayan Singing Bowls

Hand-hammered bronze bowls that produce rich, layered tones. Each bowl resonates at specific frequencies that promote deep relaxation and healing.

Gongs

Large ceremonial gongs create powerful waves of sound that wash through the body, releasing tension and creating space for transformation.

Crystal Bowls

Pure quartz crystal bowls produce clear, sustained tones that resonate with the body's energy centres, promoting alignment and clarity.
