# What is Sound Healing?

Sound healing is an ancient practice that uses vibrational frequencies to promote physical, emotional, and spiritual wellbeing. Through instruments like singing bowls, gongs, and tuning forks, practitioners create soundscapes that guide participants into deep states of relaxation.

## How Does It Work?

When you experience a sound bath, the frequencies produced by the instruments interact with your body's own vibrational field. This can help:

- Reduce stress and anxiety
- Lower blood pressure
- Improve sleep quality
- Release emotional blockages
- Enhance meditation practice

## What to Expect

During a sound healing session, you'll typically lie down in a comfortable position while the practitioner plays various instruments around you. The experience is deeply personal—some people see colors, others feel physical sensations, and many simply drift into a peaceful, meditative state.
