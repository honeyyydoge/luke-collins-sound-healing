Corporate Wellness

Sound healing for the modern workplace

Bring Sound Healing to Your Workplace

In today's high-pressure corporate environment, employee wellbeing isn't optional—it's essential. Sound healing offers a unique, accessible way to reduce workplace stress and support your team.

From lunchtime sessions to full wellness days, Luke Collins brings the transformative power of Himalayan gongs and singing bowls directly to your office, boardroom, or offsite location.

Benefits for Your Team

Stress Reduction

Sound frequencies activate the parasympathetic nervous system, rapidly lowering cortisol and calming the mind.

Improved Focus

Employees return to work with clearer thinking, better concentration, and enhanced decision-making ability.

Team Cohesion

Shared wellness experiences build connection and trust among team members.

Reduced Burnout

Regular sound healing sessions help prevent burnout and support long-term employee retention.

Corporate Offerings

Lunchtime Sessions

45-minute sound baths perfect for the lunch break. Employees lie down, reset their nervous systems, and return to work refreshed.

Wellness Days

Full or half-day programs combining sound healing with other wellness modalities. Ideal for team offsites and company wellness initiatives.

Regular Programs

Weekly or monthly sessions to support ongoing employee wellbeing. Build sound healing into your workplace culture.

Executive Sessions

Private sound healing for leadership teams facing high-pressure periods or major decisions.
