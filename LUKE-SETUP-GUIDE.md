# 🎵 Luke's Quick Setup Guide

Hey Luke! This guide will help you get found by more people searching for sound healing in Sydney. It takes about 30-45 minutes total.

---

## Step 1: Google Business Profile (Most Important!)

This is how you show up when people search "sound healing near me" or "sound bath Balmain".

### How to set it up:

1. Go to **business.google.com**
2. Click "Manage now"
3. Sign in with a Google account (or create one)
4. Click "Add your business"
5. Enter: **Luke Collins Sound Healing**
6. Category: Search for "Sound Healing Practitioner" or "Meditation Center"
7. Add your address: **37 Nicholson Street, Balmain NSW 2041**
8. Add phone number (if you want calls)
9. Add website: (your website URL once live)
10. Verify your business (Google will send a postcard OR call you)

### After verification, add:
- ✅ Photos (your gongs, bowls, garden, you in action)
- ✅ Business hours
- ✅ Services with prices ($50 group, $120 private)
- ✅ Description (copy from your website About page)

**Time: 15 minutes + wait for verification**

## Step 2: Get Google Reviews

Reviews help you rank higher AND build trust.

### Ask past clients:
Send them this message:

> "Hey [Name]! If you enjoyed the sound bath session, I'd really appreciate a quick Google review. It helps other people find me. Here's the link: [your Google review link]"

**Goal: Get 5 reviews in the next 2 weeks**

### How to find your review link:
1. Google "Luke Collins Sound Healing"
2. Click your business listing
3. Click "Write a review" 
4. Copy that URL
5. Share with clients

## Step 3: Submit to Directories (15 minutes)

Create free listings on these sites. Just add your basic info + link to website.

### Priority 1 (Do these first):

**Natural Therapy Pages** (Aussie wellness directory)
→ naturaltherapypages.com.au/register
→ Add: Sound Healing, Balmain, your services

**Yelp Australia**
→ biz.yelp.com.au
→ Claim or add your business

**Eventbrite** (for your events)
→ eventbrite.com.au
→ Create organizer account
→ List your sound bath events

### Priority 2 (Do when you have time):

- **True Local** → truelocal.com.au
- **Yellow Pages** → yellowpages.com.au
- **Hotfrog** → hotfrog.com.au
- **Facebook Page** → facebook.com/pages/create

### What to put on each:
- Business name: Luke Collins Sound Healing
- Address: 37 Nicholson Street, Balmain NSW 2041
- Phone: [your number]
- Website: [your URL]
- Category: Sound Healing / Meditation / Wellness
- Description: "Sound healing practitioner offering gong baths and singing bowl sessions in Balmain, Sydney. Group sessions from $50, private sessions $120."
